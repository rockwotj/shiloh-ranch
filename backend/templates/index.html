Delete

If something is deleted from the Wordpress site, it is not removed from the app!

In order to delete something from the app, simply go to View Data in the side panel, then click on the category of what you will be deleting. Then from that page simply find the data that you would like to delete and press the red delete button.

WARNING! There is no way recover deleted data.
